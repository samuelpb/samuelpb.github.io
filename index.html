Introduction

In the fall of my senior year at Rutgers' University in New Brunswick, New Jersey, I recieved an offer to work at Amazon as a Software Development Engineer, and it has brought me all the way accross the country here to Seattle. I know I have a long journey ahead of me, but I am excited for what is to come.

Growing up, my main hobby was soccer, playing for clubs and for my highschool. I ended up playing some club soccer at Rutgers', but ultimately focused on my education. Starting as a Molecular Biology major, I learned after about a year that my passion was in the logic and way of thinking that comes with programming. From there, I switched to start earning my computer science degree, as well as a minor in mathematics and cognitive science.

Aside from work, I spend a decent amount of the time at the gym, as it is my way to relax and clear my mind. Elsewhere, I constantly am cooking, or learning more to play on the guitar. And, every once in a while, nothing beats a week scuba-diving.
